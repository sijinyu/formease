# FormEase - One-Click Form Auto-Filler

Tired of typing the same name, email, and phone number into every form?
FormEase saves your profile once and fills any web form with a single click.

## Features

- **One-Click Fill** — Fill 20+ fields (name, email, phone, address, etc.) instantly
- **Keyboard Shortcuts** — Ctrl+Shift+F to fill immediately, Ctrl+Shift+E to preview (Mac: Cmd)
- **Undo** — Filled something wrong? One click to restore original values
- **Auto-Save** — Profile edits are saved automatically. No save button needed
- **Export/Import** — Back up your profile as JSON and move it to another browser

## Why FormEase?

- **No account required** — Zero sign-up friction
- **No server** — All data stays in your browser. Nothing is ever uploaded
- **100% Free** — No hidden fees, no premium tier
- **No AI** — Lightweight and fast. Works offline

## Perfect For

- Anyone who fills the same info on multiple sites daily
- Job seekers filling dozens of application forms
- Privacy-conscious users who don't want data leaving their browser

## Supported Fields

First name, Last name, Full name, Email, Phone, Birth date, Gender, Zip code, Address, Address detail, City, Company, Department, Position, Career, Bio, Website, GitHub, LinkedIn

Recognizes both Korean and English form fields.
